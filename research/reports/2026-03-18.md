# Daily AI Research Summary — 2026-03-18

## Hallucination & Reliability

1. **Is Conformal Factuality for RAG-based LLMs Robust?** (arXiv, March 2026)
   - Explores conformal factuality as a statistical guarantee for RAG-based systems
   - Addresses the gap between retrieval grounding and actual output factuality
   - Authors: Yi Chen, Daiwei Chen, et al.

2. **Kestrel: Grounding Self-Refinement for LVLM Hallucination Mitigation** (arXiv, March 2026)
   - Targets multimodal hallucination in large vision-language models
   - Proposes self-refinement grounded in visual evidence
   - Authors: Jiawei Mao, Haoqin Tu, Huaxiu Yao, et al.

3. **Segmentation-Based Attention Entropy: Detecting and Mitigating Object Hallucinations in LVLMs** (arXiv, March 2026)
   - Novel entropy-based method to detect object hallucinations
   - Focuses on text modality contributions to visual hallucinations

4. **DynHD: Hallucination Detection for Diffusion LLMs** (arXiv, March 2026)
   - Addresses hallucinations in emerging diffusion-based language models
   - Uses denoising dynamics deviation learning for detection

5. **SFCoT: Safer Chain-of-Thought via Active Safety Evaluation and Calibration** (arXiv, March 2026)
   - Calibrates reasoning steps in CoT to prevent jailbreak vulnerabilities
   - Monitors intermediate reasoning, not just final outputs
