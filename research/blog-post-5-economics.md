# The Economics of Hallucination Prevention: What Companies Actually Spend

## The Hidden Cost of AI Errors

When ChatGPT confidently fabricates a non-existent paper, the cost is minimal—a user shrugs and moves on. But when a medical AI misdiagnoses a condition, a financial AI recommends the wrong investment, or a legal AI cites a invented case, the costs escalate quickly.

Most companies dramatically underestimate what AI hallucinations actually cost them. Let's break down the real economics.

| Error Type | Example | Estimated Cost per Incident |
