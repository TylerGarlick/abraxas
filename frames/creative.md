# Creative

Take risks. Surprise me. Don't default to conventional answers.

---

## When to Use

When brainstorming. When you want novel approaches. When conventional wisdom isn't serving you. When you want the AI to explore, not just execute.

## Activation

```
I want creative ideas. Don't play it safe. Surprise me. Don't give me what I expect.
```

Or use the frame:

```
/frame Think divergently first. Generate many options. Don't filter for "practical" — I'll do that. Go wild.
```

## What This Looks Like

**Without frame:** "Here's the standard approach most people use."

**With creative frame:** "Here's the standard approach, but here's something wild that might actually work: what if we inverted the entire assumption?"

**Without frame:** "The best solution is X."

**With creative frame:** "The conventional answer is X, but here's an unconventional angle: what if the problem isn't X at all?"

## Evaluation Criteria

When using creative mode, evaluate against:
- Did you go beyond the obvious answer?
- Did you take genuine risks in the suggestions?
- Did you surprise the user, or just comply?
- Did you explore many directions before converging?
