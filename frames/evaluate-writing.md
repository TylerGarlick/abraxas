# Evaluate: Writing

Check: clarity, audience fit, tone consistency, structural flow. Mark what needs work.

---

## When to Use

When reviewing written content. When you need feedback on effectiveness, not just correctness. When audience reception matters.

## Activation

```
Review this writing. I want to know: is it clear? Does it fit the audience? Is the tone right? What needs work?
```

Or use the frame:

```
/frame Evaluate this for clarity, audience appropriateness, tone consistency, and structural flow. Tell me what works and what doesn't. Don't just proofread — assess effectiveness.
```

## Evaluation Criteria

When evaluating writing, assess against:

| Dimension | What to Look For |
|-----------|------------------|
| Clarity | Are ideas expressed unambiguously? Any confusing passages? |
| Audience Fit | Right level for intended reader? Assumes right background? |
| Tone | Consistent? Appropriate for context? Matches brand voice? |
| Structure | Logical flow? Transitions work? Headings clear? |
| Impact | Does it achieve its purpose? Where does it lose readers? |

## What This Looks Like

**Without criteria:** "Good writing."

**With evaluate-writing:** "This is clear in sections 1-2, but section 3 gets muddled — you're trying to do too much in one paragraph. Also, your tone shifts from professional to casual in the middle, which creates cognitive dissonance for the reader."

**Without criteria:** "I liked it."

**With evaluate-writing:** "Your opening hooks well, but the call-to-action at the end is weak. You're asking the reader to do something, but you haven't given them enough reason to act."

## Output Format

Structure your evaluation as:

```
## Overall Assessment
[Does this achieve its purpose?]

## Clarity Issues
- [Passage that confuses + why]
- [Fix suggestion]

## Audience Fit
- [Gap between writer assumption and reader reality]

## Tone Consistency
- [Where tone shifts inappropriately]

## Structural Issues
- [Flow problems, missing transitions, unclear organization]

## High-Impact Improvements
- [What would most improve effectiveness]

## Verdict
[Ready to publish / needs revision / needs rewrite]
```

## Success Criteria

- Did you assess effectiveness, not just correctness?
- Did you identify specific passages needing work?
- Did you consider the actual audience, not an abstract one?
- Did you prioritize improvements by impact?
- Did you distinguish between style preference and clarity issue?
