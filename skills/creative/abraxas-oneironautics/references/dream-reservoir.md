## SYNCHRONICITY NODES

Synchronicities are logged as a distinct node type in the Dream Reservoir.
They are received without interpretation — prima materia held in witnessing.

### Schema

| Field | Description |
|:---|:---|
| `Sync_ID` | Unique identifier (e.g., `SYNC_001`) |
| `Date` | Date the coincidence was noticed |
| `Outer Event` | What happened in waking reality |
| `Inner Symbol` | What symbol or figure was active in the dream/symbolic field |
| `Notes` | Optional: how it was noticed, any felt sense — not interpretation |

### Example Entry

```
SYNC_001 | 2026-02-27 | Found a dead bird on the threshold of the house
         | {The Threshold} symbol — active from previous session
         | Notes: noticed the coincidence without reaching for meaning. Logged.
```

### Synchronicity Edge Types

Synchronicity nodes may form edges with symbol nodes if patterns emerge across multiple logged events:

- `--[RESONATES_WITH]-->` — A synchronicity node resonates with a Dream Reservoir symbol
  (both the outer event and inner symbol share quality or image — no causal claim)
- `--[AMPLIFIED_BY]-->` — A symbol in the Reservoir is amplified by a synchronicity;
  the outer event adds weight or urgency to the inner symbol without explaining it

### AMPLIFIED_BY Edge

The `AMPLIFIED_BY` edge is also available for connections between symbols and
amplification work (e.g., output from `/amplify {symbol}` or `/myth {figure}`):

- `{Symbol} --[AMPLIFIED_BY]--> {Amplification_Node}` — Sol and Nox amplification
  material attached to a symbol without altering the symbol's existing content

### RESONATES_WITH Edge

The `RESONATES_WITH` edge connects two symbols that share quality, image, or felt sense
without implying identity or causality:

- `{Symbol_A} --[RESONATES_WITH]--> {Symbol_B}` — emerged from `/resonate` analysis
  or from pattern recognition across sessions

Both edge types are observational. They record structural relationships without
asserting meaning. Meaning belongs to the practitioner.
