Solomon's Gate

Abraxas

Six systems, six failure modes addressed.

All forces united against hallucination, scheming, and unanchored confidence.
Honest
Anti-Hallucination
Labels every claim as known, inferred, uncertain, or unknown. Makes hallucination visible and resistible.
Logos
Socratic Analysis
Surfaces hidden assumptions and traces inferential chains. Makes reasoning explicit and interrogable.
Agon
Adversarial Debate
Forces genuine position asymmetry. Finds where disagreement is real and where it's just performance.
Janus
Meta-Cognition
Maintains the Sol/Nox boundary — factual vs. symbolic. Prevents mixing evidence-grounded claims with imaginative content.
Aletheia
Anti-Obfuscation
Forces plain language. Resists hedging, nominalization, and doublespeak. Truth requires directness.
Logos-Math
Mathematical Verification New
Verifies AI math output through script execution. Catches arithmetic errors, algebraic mistakes, and misapplied formulas.

Installation

Skills are personal-scope installations. Unzip each .skill archive into your Claude Code skills directory:

unzip honest.skill -d ~/.claude/skills/
unzip logos.skill -d ~/.claude/skills/
unzip agon.skill -d ~/.claude/skills/
unzip janus-system.skill -d ~/.claude/skills/
unzip aletheia.skill -d ~/.claude/skills/
unzip logos-math.skill -d ~/.claude/skills/

Don't have Claude Code? Load CONSTITUTION.md as your system prompt in any LLM — every system activates without installation.
